What you need to bring to pick-up
ID requirements
A valid passport is required.
Driver's license requirements

At pick-up, the main driver and any additional drivers must present their original driver's licenses and required types of proof. Electronic versions, photos, or non-official licenses (temporary, learner's, or regionally restricted) will not be accepted.

All drivers must have held their licenses for at least 12 months. If your new license does not show the date first issued, bring your old license to prove driving experience.

Drivers age requirements: 25–80 years old.

Young drivers may be subject to a surcharge. Taxes and fees should be paid online or at the branch.

Check with your rental branch regarding age requirements and charges for additional drivers.

The branch accepts the following driver's licenses and types of proof:
Original Driving License + International Driving Permit

Original Driving License: Original full valid driving license issued by the main driver's country.

International Driving Permit (IDP): Proof of a driving certificate issued by a member of the United Nations Convention on Road Traffic. A 1949 Geneva Convention IDP is valid for 1 year; a 1968 Vienna Convention IDP is valid for 3 years. The passport, driver's license, and IDP must all be issued by the same country/region.

Payment at pick-up
A valid credit card in the main driver's name is required as a security deposit. Debit cards and prepaid cards are not accepted.
Fuel policy
Full to Full: The car is provided with a full tank. Return it full to avoid additional charges.
Mileage
Unlimited mileage included. No extra charges for distance driven.
