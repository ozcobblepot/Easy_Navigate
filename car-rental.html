What you need to bring to pick-up
ID requirements

Driver's license requirements

At pick-up, the main driver and any additional drivers must present their original driver's licenses and required types of proof. Electronic versions, photos, or non-official licenses will not be accepted.

All drivers must have held their licenses for at least 12 months.

Drivers age requirements: 25–80 years old.

The branch accepts: Original Driving License + International Driving Permit

Original Driving License: Original full valid driving license issued by the main driver's country.

International Driving Permit (IDP): Proof of a driving certificate issued by a member of the United Nations Convention on Road Traffic.

Payment at pick-up
A valid credit card in the main driver's name is required as a security deposit. Debit cards and prepaid cards are not accepted.
